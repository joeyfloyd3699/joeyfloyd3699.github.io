Lumina — Brand Identity System

A comprehensive visual identity for a luxury lighting studio. The project encompassed logo design, typography selection, color systems, and a full suite of brand collateral — from business cards to environmental signage.

The design language draws from the interplay of light and shadow, using warm metallics against deep, matte surfaces to evoke a sense of refined craftsmanship.

Rosé — Editorial Design

Art direction and layout design for an independent fashion and culture magazine. Each issue explores the tension between softness and structure through experimental typography and photography.

The editorial system uses a modular grid that allows for dramatic full-bleed imagery alongside intimate, tightly-set text columns, creating a rhythm that mirrors the magazine's dual nature.
